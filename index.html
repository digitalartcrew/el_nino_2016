About El Niño Training Center

El Niño Training Center is a top premiere training and fitness facility in mixed martial arts. We cater to all levels whether new to the sport or training for a professional competition. Our style is constantly evolving based on practices founded in: American & Braziilian Jiu Jitsu, Wooden Man Muay Thai, Wrestling, Boxing – and optimized by Athletic Performance Training and Sports Medicine.

Although our coaches are elite in their respected fields, they share a passion for understanding the art of combat as a whole. Our edge is in how we integrate these disciplines, preserving their authenticity while aligning them towards a single, adaptable way of fighting.

Classes

Achieve the best results with our wide variety of training options!

Fitness

Fitness classes enhance balance, speed, mobility, strength, power and agility. Learning how to control the body and the mind during intense training sessions allows for maximum performance and resistance to injury.

Jujitsu

Our gym has a team of Gracie Black Belt and advanced instructors skilled in effective submission techniques. For the more aggressive grappler, we have implented American Jiu Jitsu, designed by UFC veteran Jake Shields.

Boxing

Boxing is a great sport for developing hand-eye coordination and effective striking. Our program starts from the basics of learning how to throw a jab, straight, hook and uppercut then advancing those techniques to develop combinations.

Muay Thai

Our program is a unique opportunity to learn from one of Thailand’s legends, Jongsanan ‘The Wooden Man’. Woodenman Muay Thai is based in solid technique, intelligent strategy, tough conditioning and balance.

Wrestling

Wrestling is one of the oldest forms of Olympic competition used to control opponents by securing body locks, throws and leg attacks. We focus on mat control, explosive takedowns, set ups and controlling the pummel.

Youth

Our youth programs support both the fundamentals of wrestling and Brazilian Jiu Jitsu. We teach the fundamentals techniques, while implementing safety and developing self-discipline.

SKrap Pack

El Niño Training Center is home of the "Skrap Pack", which is one of the top MMA competitions teams across the globe. Many of our team members hold national and world titles which requires intense training to accomplish.

MMA

MMA is a combination of several mixed martial arts elements consisting of striking and grappling. This courses teaches fighters how to successfully integrate these styles in a live scenario taught by UFC veteran, Josh Clopton.

Mobility

Mobility exercise is for finding balance, focus, strength and flexibilty.
